Top 3 Movies of 2015-2016

In this website I will tell all of you what I believe to be the Top 3 movies of 2015-2016. This list will not specify any genre.
.

3. Doctor Strange (2016)

This movie is believed to be one of the many Marvel Masterpieces, Doctor Starnge delievers to Marvels amazing standard. The stunning visuals , the amazing storyline and the wonderful sfx will make for an unforgettable experiance in the cinema

2. Furious 7 (2015)

The seventh installment in box office hit was sure to live up to its expectations. Vin Diesal once again suprised us with his supreme acting. This movie is also very special because it serves as a mommento to one of the actors , Paul Walker, who had passed away just before the premiere of the movie

1. Star Wars The Force Awakens (2015)

A highly anticipated movie and the start of a new chapter for the star wars franchise it self. The movie folllows the story of Rey , a young women who lives on a desert planet. When selling parts that she found from an old ship , she meets Finn , a storm trooper who had gone rouge. Together they adventure through the galaxy. The reason that this movie takes my number 1 spot is because of the amazing storyline, visuals and acting.
